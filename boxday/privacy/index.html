Privacy Policy

Boxday
Last updated: May 31, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Boxday stores the information you enter in the app, including time box titles, start times, durations, completion status, recurrence settings, and notification preferences. Boxday does not require an account and does not collect your name, email address, contacts, location, photos, or payment card details.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used to provide Boxday's core features, including scheduling time boxes, showing your daily plan, sending local reminders, tracking completion, and restoring your data from backups when you choose to use backup features.

3. Data Storage

Your time box data is stored locally on your device using Apple's local storage technologies. Boxday does not operate its own server for your time box content. If you use iCloud Backup, backup files are stored in your personal iCloud account through Apple's iCloud Drive service.

4. Notifications

Boxday uses standard local notifications to remind you about time boxes. These notifications are scheduled on your device. Boxday does not use push notifications or Critical Alerts.

5. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Boxday may offer paid subscriptions or in-app purchases. Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. Subscription status may be managed through RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management service. Boxday does not have direct access to your payment card details.

6. Third-Party Services

Boxday may use Apple services, including the App Store, StoreKit, local notifications, and iCloud Drive. Boxday may also use RevenueCat to manage subscription status and purchase restoration. Boxday does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not sell your personal data.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your time box content with third parties except as needed to provide services you choose to use, such as iCloud Backup through Apple. Subscription-related information may be processed by Apple and RevenueCat for purchase management, entitlement checks, and restore purchases.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Data stored locally remains on your device until you delete it in the app or delete the app from your device. iCloud backup files remain in your iCloud account until you delete them or manage them through iCloud. Subscription records are managed by Apple and RevenueCat according to their respective policies.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact woorlds@gmail.com.
