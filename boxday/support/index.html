Subscriptions

To cancel or manage your subscription, open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your Apple ID, then go to Subscriptions.

Data and Backup

Boxday stores your time box data on your device. If you use iCloud Backup, your backup files are stored in your personal iCloud account. You can manage or delete local app data by deleting entries in the app or deleting the app from your device.

Notifications

Boxday uses local notifications for time box reminders. If reminders are not appearing, check iPhone Settings > Notifications > Boxday and confirm that notifications are allowed.
