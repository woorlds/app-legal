Privacy Policy

MindTrade
Last updated: May 31, 2026

1. Information We Collect

MindTrade stores the information you enter in the app, including trade records, ticker symbols, asset types, trade direction and outcome, emotion scores, confidence levels, notes, bias tags, hold-reason tags, and monthly report data. MindTrade does not require an account and does not collect your name, email address, contacts, location, photos, or payment card details.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used to provide MindTrade's core features, including trade journaling, psychology tracking, bias analysis, insight generation, monthly reports, and data backup or restore when you choose to use backup features.

3. Data Storage

Your trading journal data is stored locally on your device using Apple's local storage technologies. MindTrade does not operate its own server for your trading journal content. If you use iCloud Backup, backup files are stored in your personal iCloud account through Apple's iCloud service.

4. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

MindTrade may offer paid subscriptions or in-app purchases. Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. Subscription status may be managed through RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management service. MindTrade does not have direct access to your payment card details.

5. Third-Party Services

MindTrade may use Apple services, including the App Store, StoreKit, and iCloud. MindTrade may also use RevenueCat to manage subscription status and purchase restoration. MindTrade does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not sell your personal data.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your trading journal content with third parties except as needed to provide services you choose to use, such as iCloud Backup through Apple. Subscription-related information may be processed by Apple and RevenueCat for purchase management, entitlement checks, and restore purchases.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Data stored locally remains on your device until you delete it in the app or delete the app from your device. iCloud backup files remain in your iCloud account until you delete them or manage them through iCloud. Subscription records are managed by Apple and RevenueCat according to their respective policies.
