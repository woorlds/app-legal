Subscriptions

To cancel or manage your subscription, open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your Apple ID, then go to Subscriptions.

Data and Backup

MindTrade stores your trading journal data on your device. If you use iCloud Backup, your backup files are stored in your personal iCloud account. You can delete local app data by deleting entries in the app or deleting the app from your device.
